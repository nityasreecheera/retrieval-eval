People Operations: Time Off Policy
Last Updated: August 1, 2024
Owner: Maya Torres, Head of People

Vacation and PTO:
All full-time employees accrue 20 days of paid time off per year, accrued at 1.67 days per month starting from the first day of employment. PTO does not roll over at the end of the calendar year — unused days are forfeited on December 31. Employees are encouraged to take at least 10 days per year. Managers should flag team members who have not taken any PTO by October 1 each year.

Sick Leave:
Employees receive 10 sick days per year, separate from PTO. Sick leave does not accrue and resets on January 1. Sick leave can be used for personal illness, caring for an immediate family member, or medical appointments. Unused sick leave is not paid out upon separation from the company.

Parental Leave:
Primary caregivers receive 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave. Secondary caregivers receive 6 weeks of fully paid parental leave. Leave must be taken within 12 months of the birth, adoption, or foster placement. Employees should notify People Ops at least 30 days before the expected start of leave when possible.

Company Holidays:
The company observes 11 federal holidays plus a floating holiday that employees may use on any day of their choosing. The office closes for a winter break from December 25 through January 1 each year; these days do not count against PTO.

Requesting Time Off:
All time off requests must be submitted in Rippling at least 5 business days in advance for requests of 3 or more consecutive days. Shorter requests should still be submitted in Rippling and communicated directly to your manager. Final approval rests with the direct manager.
